# Work Plan: Project ClubOS (Group E)

## 1. Project Identity

- **Consultancy Name:** Off-Pitch Analytics
- **Product Name:** ClubOS
- **Project Theme:** Group E - Historical Digital Business Data

## 2. Project Objective

Off-Pitch Analytics proposes **ClubOS**, a recurring monthly digital business operating system for the club's business and digital teams.

Rather than delivering only a static dashboard, the project will transform the provided historical datasets into a reusable product workflow that can be refreshed each month as new files arrive.

The MVP will focus on four product modules:

1. **Priority Board** - ranked monthly issues and opportunities
2. **Command Center** - health view across Website, eCommerce, Streaming, and Fan App
3. **Peer Benchmark Engine** - Real Madrid vs peer clubs on benchmark-supported KPIs
4. **Commercial Signal Engine** - selected 1-3 month leading relationships tied to commercial performance

A supporting visualization layer will also be prepared for the formal reporting requirement.

## 3. Delivery Approach

The project will be delivered through three connected workstreams:

- **Data Platform Workstream:** build a reproducible Databricks pipeline using a Bronze / Silver / Gold structure for recurring monthly refreshes
- **Product Workstream:** design and prototype the ClubOS application experience around a decision-support workflow, not a chart collection
- **Reporting Workstream:** prepare a clean companion BI layer for summary and drill-down reporting where required

This approach keeps the project aligned with the official visualization brief while extending it into a more advanced and reusable SaaS-style solution.

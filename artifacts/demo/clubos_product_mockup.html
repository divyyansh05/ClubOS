Screen Explainer

Priority Board

This is the first screen because it answers the most important business question: what deserves attention first this month?

What this screen contains

  • Top summary strip with counts of critical priorities and opportunities
  • Ranked cards with score, category, why it matters, and suggested next investigation
  • Supporting right rail with anomaly, benchmark note, and active signal

What the user learns

  • Which issue matters most right now
  • What evidence supports that issue
  • Whether it is a benchmark problem, conversion problem, or opportunity

What to say in the meeting

  • This is the hero module because it changes the product from reporting to decision support.
  • Everything else exists to explain and support this ranked list.
Use this page for group alignment: “this is what the product will look like,” not “this is the final implemented UI.”
